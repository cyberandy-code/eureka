# ✨ Daily Task Tracker - Kawaii Edition ✨

A beautiful, pastel, and adorable daily task tracking app with analytics, progress tracking, and daily motivational quotes! Track your tasks and celebrate your progress every single day. 🌸💕

## 🌈 Features

### 📋 Task Management
- ✨ Add, complete, and delete tasks with ease
- 💾 Auto-saves all your tasks throughout the day
- 🗑️ Quick delete button for each task
- ✅ Check off tasks as you complete them
- 📱 Responsive design that works on all devices

### 💭 Daily Mood & Summary
- 😌 Select your daily mood (Motivated, Neutral, Tired, Excited)
- 📝 Write a qualitative summary of what you accomplished
- 💾 Save your summary with one click
- 🌟 Track mood patterns over time

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard
- 📈 Daily progress percentage calculation
- 🔥 Current streak tracker for consistent productivity
- 📊 Weekly progress chart with Chart.js
- 📅 Last 7 days history overview
- 😊 Mood tracker to see your emotional patterns

### 🎯 Daily Motivation
- ✨ New inspirational quote every single day
- 💕 25 unique motivational messages
- 🦋 Cute, encouraging, and positive vibes only!

### 🎨 Design
- 💗 Pastel pink, blue, lavender, and mint color palette
- 🦄 Cute kawaii aesthetic with emoji decorations
- ✨ Smooth animations and transitions
- 🌸 Floating background decorations
- 📱 Mobile-friendly and responsive

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open** `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Add tasks** by typing in the input field and clicking "Add" (or pressing Enter)
3. **Track your mood** using the mood selector dropdown
4. **Check off tasks** as you complete them throughout the day
5. **Write a summary** of your day in the summary textarea
6. **View analytics** in real-time on the right side
7. **Watch your progress** grow over days and weeks!

## 📁 Files

- `index.html` - Main HTML structure with all UI elements
- `styles.css` - Beautiful pastel kawaii styling
- `script.js` - All functionality and data management

## 💾 Data Storage

All your data is saved locally in your browser using localStorage:
- ✅ Tasks are saved as you add/complete them
- 📝 Summary is saved when you click the save button
- 😊 Mood is saved automatically
- 📊 Historical data is stored for analytics (up to 30 days)
- 🔄 Automatically detects when it's a new day and archives previous data

## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Daily Reset**: When you visit on a new day, your previous day's data is automatically saved to history
2. **Progress Calculation**: Automatically calculates what percentage of today's tasks are complete
3. **Streak Tracking**: Counts consecutive days where you completed 100% of your tasks
4. **Analytics**: Uses Chart.js to create a beautiful weekly progress chart
5. **Mood Tracking**: Stores your daily mood and displays patterns
